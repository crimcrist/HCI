AGENT PHOENIX

About Jett

Representing South Korea, Jett’s agile and evasive fighting style lets her take risks no one else can. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies down before they even know what hit them. Fast, fearless, and deadly—she's wind in human form.

Abilities:

  • Cloudburst: Instantly throw a projectile that expands into a brief vision-blocking cloud on impact.
  • Updraft: Propels Jett high into the air with a gust of wind.
  • Tailwind: Dash a short distance in the direction she’s moving.
  • Blade Storm (Ultimate): Equip a set of deadly throwing knives that deal high damage and reset on kill.
