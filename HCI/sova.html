AGENT PHOENIX

About Sova

Raised in the icy tundra of Russia, Sova tracks, finds, and eliminates enemies with ruthless precision. Armed with recon technology and a custom bow, he sees what others can’t and strikes before they can react. Wherever you hide, Sova will find you.

Abilities:

  • Owl Drone: Deploy a pilotable drone that can fire a dart to reveal enemies it hits.
  • Shock Bolt: Fire an explosive bolt that emits a damaging pulse of energy on impact.
  • Recon Bolt: Fires a bolt that activates to reveal nearby enemies caught in its line of sight.
  • Hunter’s Fury (Ultimate): Fire up to three long-range energy blasts through walls that damage and reveal enemies.
