Reporting Disruptive Players

Reporting problematic players is crucial for maintaining a fair and healthy gaming environment. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the Career menu and select Match History.
  2. Choose the relevant match.
  3. Click the name of the player to report.
  4. Provide detailed and accurate information.
  5. Explain the problem (toxicity, cheating, harassment, etc.).
  6. Include supporting evidence (screenshots or recordings).
  7. Send the report for review.

Why Report Disruptive Players?

  • Maintains a healthy gaming environment.
  • Prevents unacceptable behavior.
  • Enhances gameplay experience.

Reporting Tips

  • Provide accurate and complete information.
  • Attach strong evidence.
  • Report incidents promptly.

By reporting disruptive players, you contribute to a positive and enjoyable gaming community.
