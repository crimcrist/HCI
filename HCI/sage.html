AGENT PHOENIX

About Sage

A stronghold of China, Sage creates safety for herself and her team wherever she goes. Able to revive fallen allies and hold back aggressive pushes, she’s the calm in the chaos—a true guardian on the battlefield.

Abilities:

  • Barrier Orb: Creates a solid wall that can be rotated before casting.
  • Slow Orb: Casts a field that slows and grounds players who move through it.
  • Healing Orb: Heals herself or an ally over time.
  • Resurrection (Ultimate): Revives a dead teammate with full health.
